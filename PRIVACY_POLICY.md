# Privacy Policy

**Do I Want To Know**
Last updated: May 2026

---

## What this app is

Do I Want To Know is a survey platform. You can post questions, answer questions posted by others, and see aggregated results. The app is designed to work without requiring any personal information.

---

## What we collect

### What we do collect
- **A random device ID** — when you first open the app, a random identifier (UUID) is generated on your device and stored locally. This is used to associate your questions and answers with each other. It contains no personal information and was never an email address, name, or anything you provided.
- **Questions you post** — the text of questions you write, along with their answer type and options.
- **Answers you submit** — your selected answer to questions in the feed.
- **Timestamps** — when questions were posted and when answers were submitted.

### What we do NOT collect
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number
- Your location
- Your contacts or address book
- Any information from other apps on your device
- Browsing history
- Photos, camera, or microphone access

---

## How we use your data

- To show your questions to other users in the feed
- To record your answers so you are not shown the same question twice
- To calculate and display aggregate results (e.g. "67% said Yes")
- To show you the questions you have posted and their results

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

---

## Who we share data with

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Your questions and answers are stored on our servers. We use standard cloud infrastructure providers to host the backend. These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate data processing agreements.

Aggregate, anonymised results (e.g. "82 people answered this question, 60% said Yes") may be visible to any user who answers a question.

---

## Data retention

- Questions and answers are retained for as long as the app is operational.
- If you would like your data deleted, contact us at the address below and provide your device ID (found in the app settings). We will delete all questions and answers associated with your ID within 30 days.

---

## Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app and you would like their data removed, contact us.

---

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify users through the app.
